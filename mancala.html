My JavaScript Mancala Game!

HTML, CSS, and JavaScript

With this game I added the test cases to determine whether or not it was player one or two's turn, if there was a winner, or if the piece being passed around landed in an empty spot. If you land in an empty spot, this removes the opponents pieces directly accross the board and adds them to your Mancala (store) or if the last piece landed in the store, you are allowed to go again. These rules are based on the actual game's rules.
