The Driver's App

Ruby on Rails

The Driver's App is designed to allow drivers to create an account (or use their Google Account!), Log in, Log out, and add their upcoming races all around the world, as well as their cars.

This app is intuitive enough to show which drivers have driven each specific car, as well as which cars have been used in each race dynamically.

You can add a race to a pre-existing car, but you cannot create a car by adding it to a race.

You can also delete or update a race, or a specific car.
